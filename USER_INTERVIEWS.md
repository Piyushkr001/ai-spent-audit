# USER_INTERVIEWS.md

## Project: SpendLens AI

SpendLens AI is a free AI spend audit tool for startup founders, CTOs, engineering managers, and AI-heavy teams. The goal of these interviews is to understand how real users manage AI tool spending, whether they trust automated savings estimates, and what would make them take action after seeing an audit.

> Important: These interviews must be based on real conversations. Do not fabricate names, companies, quotes, or insights. Fabricated interviews should not be submitted.

---

# Interview Summary

## Target interview profile

The target users for SpendLens AI are:

- Startup founders
- CTOs
- Engineering managers
- Indie hackers
- AI-heavy developers
- Agency owners using multiple AI tools
- Teams paying for Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Gemini, Windsurf, or v0

## Interview goals

These interviews focus on:

1. Which AI tools users currently pay for.
2. Whether users know their exact monthly AI spend.
3. Whether users believe they are overpaying.
4. Whether plan recommendations are trusted.
5. Whether a shareable audit report is useful.
6. Whether users would submit an email after seeing savings.
7. Whether high-savings users would consider a Credex consultation.

---

# Interview Questions

Use these questions during each interview:

1. Which AI tools do you or your team currently use?
2. Which tools are paid, and which are free?
3. Roughly how much do you spend per month on AI tools?
4. Do you know if your current plans are the right fit for your usage?
5. Have you ever downgraded, cancelled, or switched an AI tool because of cost?
6. What would make you trust an automated AI spend audit?
7. Would a per-tool savings breakdown be useful?
8. Would you share an audit report with your team, founder, or finance person?
9. Would you enter your email after seeing the audit result?
10. If the tool showed more than $500/month in potential savings, would you consider a consultation or credit review?
