# Economics

### Estimate Converted Lead Value
Credex generates revenue through consulting and reselling discounted AI infrastructure credits. A typical Series B startup spending $100k/yr on AI infrastructure could save 20% ($20k) through Credex. Assuming Credex takes a 30% cut of the savings or charges a flat implementation fee, the average Lifetime Value (LTV) of a successfully closed lead is estimated at **$6,000**.

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel
Since SpendLens AI acts as an automated top-of-funnel lead generator, the marginal cost to acquire a lead through the tool is near zero.
- **Organic / Hacker News**: CAC is $0 (time invested only).
- **LinkedIn Cold Outreach**: CAC is $50-$100 (amortized cost of Sales Navigator and SDR time).
- **Paid Ads (Future)**: Assuming a $3 CPC and a 2% conversion to a qualified lead, paid CAC would be roughly $150 per qualified lead.

### Conversion Math (Audit → Consultation → Purchase)
To understand the funnel economics, we project the following conversion rates:
1. **Audits Run to Leads Generated**: 10% (Users who save the report and leave an email).
2. **Leads to High-Value Qualified Leads (MQLs)**: 20% (Leads with >$5k in identified savings).
3. **Qualified Leads to Consultation Booked**: 25% (Users who agree to jump on a call with Credex).
4. **Consultation to Closed Deal**: 30% (Credex successfully sells their services).

*Therefore, it takes approximately 666 audits to generate 1 closed deal.*
(666 audits → 66 leads → 13 MQLs → 3.3 consultations → 1 closed deal).

At an LTV of $6,000, every audit run on the platform generates an Expected Value (EV) of roughly **$9.00**.

### What Must Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months?
To generate $1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) through leads generated by SpendLens AI within 18 months, the following conditions must be met:
1. **Volume**: We need to close approximately 166 deals ($1M / $6,000 LTV).
2. **Traffic Requirements**: Based on our conversion math (666 audits per deal), the platform must process roughly 110,000 total audits over 18 months. This requires an average of ~6,000 audits per month, or ~200 per day.
3. **Virality/SEO**: We cannot achieve 200 audits/day purely on manual outreach. The tool must achieve organic SEO dominance for terms like "AI tool cost calculator" or develop a viral loop where CTOs share their "Efficiency Scores" with their peer networks.
4. **Credex Closing Rate**: The Credex sales team must maintain the assumed 30% close rate on consultations. If their close rate drops, the required top-of-funnel traffic becomes exponentially higher.
