## FAQs

**1. Is this tool really free?**  
Yes, the audit tool is 100% free. If your savings are substantial, we offer a free consultation through Credex to help you implement enterprise cost controls.

**2. Do you connect to my bank account?**  
No. SpendLens AI uses self-reported numbers. You manually input your current team size, tools, and estimated monthly spend.

**3. What AI tools do you support?**  
We currently track up-to-date pricing for major tools like Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Windsurf, and v0, as well as direct API usage.

**4. How is the data analyzed?**  
We use a deterministic, rule-based engine to find overlapping tool functions, unused seat ratios, and enterprise-tier waste. We only use AI (Google Gemini) to summarize the final results.

**5. How is my data protected?**  
Your audit is run locally in your browser first. Data is only sent to our servers if you choose to generate a saved public report.
